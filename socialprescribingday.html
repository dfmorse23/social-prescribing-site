Social Prescribing Day

Social Prescribing USA (SPUSA) is spearheading efforts for the first national celebration of social prescribing day in the United States on March 14th.

Across the country, organizations large and small will be hosting events and programs about the importance of social prescribing, what they do in their local communities to help improve the health of individuals, and building awareness of the rapidly-growing movement. SPUSA is encouraging all groups to participate in this national celebration!

In the Bay Area, SPUSA will be hosting a public program in San Francisco featuring social prescribing leaders from around the country. To learn more about that event, click here. For more information, visit Social Prescribing Day.
